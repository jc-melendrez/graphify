Features

Built for speed, designed for clarity

Everything you need to go from raw data to a polished visualization — nothing you don't.

Instant Rendering

Your chart appears the moment you select your columns. No server round-trips, no loading spinners — pure client-side rendering powered by Google Charts.

Multi-Format Support

CSV, JSON, XLSX, and XLS files all work out of the box. Smart column detection automatically separates your labels from your numbers.

Five Color Themes

From warm Sunset to cool Ocean, vibrant Neon to understated Mono — pick a palette that matches your presentation or report.

Combo Charts

Mix bars and lines on a single chart to compare different scales. Perfect for revenue vs. growth rate, temperature vs. precipitation, or any dual-metric story.

PNG Export

One click downloads your chart as a high-quality PNG. Drop it straight into a slide deck, document, or dashboard without any extra editing.

Fully Private

Your data never leaves your browser. No uploads to any server, no analytics tracking your files, no accounts storing your information. Period.

How It Works

Three steps to insight

No configuration files, no API keys, no tutorials to watch first.

1

Upload your file

Drag and drop a CSV, JSON, or Excel file onto the upload zone. Data is parsed instantly in your browser.

2

Pick your axes

Select an X-axis column and check the numeric Y-axis columns you want to visualize. Choose a chart type and color theme.

3

Export and share

Download your chart as a PNG image, or toggle the data table to inspect the underlying numbers.
